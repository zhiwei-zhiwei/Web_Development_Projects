    Budget Sunglasses:

    Under $50

    These sunglasses typically have a basic plastic frame and standard UV protection. They may not offer polarized lenses, and the build quality is generally acceptable but not exceptional. These sunglasses are great for casual use and are widely available in various styles and colors.

    Mid-Range Sunglasses:

    $50 to $200

    Sunglasses in this price range often offer better materials, such as high-quality plastic or metal frames. They usually come with polarized lenses, which reduce glare, making them ideal for driving or water sports. The UV protection is more reliable, and they may include additional coatings for scratch resistance. These sunglasses often strike a balance between quality and affordability.

    Premium Sunglasses:

    Over $200

    Premium sunglasses are typically from high-end brands. They boast superior lens and frame materials, such as ultra-lightweight metals and advanced polymers. The lenses offer excellent optical clarity, superior UV protection, and often include coatings for water and oil repellence. These sunglasses may have additional features like adjustable nose pads, specialized tints for specific activities (like golf or fishing), and a sleek, durable design.

    Our Stories

    SunRay Eyewear

    In Silicon Valley, SunRay Eyewear began as a humble startup with a big dream: to create a diverse range of sunglasses for every individual. The founders, a dynamic trio of an entrepreneur, a designer, and a marketer, started in a small garage, crafting budget-friendly sunglasses that quickly gained popularity for their affordability and style.

    The company soon expanded, introducing a mid-range line with polarized lenses and chic designs, targeting the young, fashion-conscious professionals. This range struck a chord, bridging the gap between affordability and quality.

    The real game-changer came with their premium line – luxury sunglasses featuring top-notch materials and advanced technology. This line catapulted SunRay to fame, attracting celebrities and business elites. With innovation at its core, SunRay also ventured into sustainable practices, launching eco-friendly and smart sunglasses.

    Internationally acclaimed, SunRay Eyewear became a symbol of style, innovation, and versatility. Their commitment to diversity in products and social responsibility – through philanthropic efforts and community engagement – solidified their reputation.

    Today, SunRay stands as a leader in the eyewear industry, a shining example of how innovative vision and dedication can lead to dazzling success. Their journey is not just a business triumph but a beacon of inspiration, showing how small ideas can evolve into impactful, global brands.
